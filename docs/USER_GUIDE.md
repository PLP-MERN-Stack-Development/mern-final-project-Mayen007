## 1. Getting Started

### Registration

To begin using Reviwa, you need to create an account:

1.  Navigate to the **Register** page.
2.  Fill in the required fields:
    - **Username**: Your unique public name.
    - **Email**: Your active email address.
    - **Password**: Choose a strong password.
3.  Click the **Register** button.
4.  After successful registration, you will be redirected to the login page.

### Login

Once you have an account, you can log in:

1.  Navigate to the **Login** page.
2.  Enter your **Email** and **Password**.
3.  Click the **Login** button.
4.  Upon successful login, you will be directed to your **Dashboard**.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your personal hub where you can:

- View a summary of your submitted reports.
- See recent activities and notifications.
- Quickly access options to create a new report or view the map.

## 3. Creating a Report

To report a new waste issue:

1.  From the Dashboard or the navigation bar, click on **Create Report**.
2.  Fill out the report form:
    - **Title**: A brief, descriptive title for the report (e.g., "Overflowing Bin").
    - **Description**: Provide more details about the issue, including severity, specific location notes, etc.
    - **Category**: Select the type of waste issue from the dropdown (e.g., "Illegal Dumping", "Overflowing Bin", "Hazardous Waste").
    - **Location**: You can either:
      - **Use Current Location**: If your browser allows, this will automatically pinpoint your current GPS coordinates.
      - **Select on Map**: Click on the map to manually drop a pin at the exact location of the waste.
      - **Manual Input**: Enter latitude and longitude coordinates directly.
    - **Images**: Upload one or more photos of the waste to provide visual evidence.
3.  Review your report details and click **Submit Report**.

Your report will be submitted and visible on the map and in the reports list.

## 4. Map Interaction

The Map feature allows you to visualize all reported waste issues geographically:

- **View Markers**: Each marker on the map represents a reported waste incident.
- **Click Markers**: Click on a marker to see a quick summary of the report. This will also provide an option to view detailed report information.
- **Zoom and Pan**: Navigate the map using standard zoom and pan controls to explore different areas.

## 5. Viewing and Managing Reports

- **Access Reports**: Go to the **Reports** page from the navigation bar.
- **Filter/Sort**: Use the available filters and sorting options to find specific reports (e.g., by status, category, date).
- **Report Details**: Click on any report to view its full details, including images, location, description, and status updates.
- **Admin Actions**: If you are an administrator, you will have additional options on report detail pages, such as changing report status or assigning it.

## 6. Profile Management

Manage your personal information and account settings:

1.  Click on your **Profile** icon/link in the navigation bar.
2.  Here you can:
    - View your submitted reports.
    - Update your username or email address.
    - Change your password.
    - View your current role and other account details.

## 7. Leaderboard

Visit the **Leaderboard** to see top contributors in the Reviwa community.

- **Rankings**: Users are ranked based on the number and impact of their submitted reports.
- **Motivation**: See how you compare to other users and strive to climb the ranks!
